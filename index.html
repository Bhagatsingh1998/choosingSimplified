Choosing Simplified or C.S. is web applaiction which is collection of all the schools, colleges, universities, teachers.

Users can add or comment about their schools, colleges, universities, teachers(from kidergarden level way upto university level). User-generated content makes Rate My Teacher the trafficked site for quickly researching and rating teachers, colleges and schools across the India.

Rate My Teachers is mainly built for college students. Choosing the best courses and professors is a right of every student, and connecting with peers on the site has become a key way for millions of students to navigate this process. The site does checking in with each other – their friends, their brothers, their sisters, their classmates – to figure out who's a great professor and who's one you might want to avoid.
